Pieter Brueghel (the Elder) Rembrandt Harmenszoon van Rijn
  • Born in Breda or Breugel between 1525 and 1530
  • Died in Brussels on September 9, 1569
  • Brabant Renaissance painter
  • Would go on to father many other famous “Bruegel” painters
